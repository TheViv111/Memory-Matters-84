## Services Offered

Evidence-based psychiatric care tailored for older adults:

1. **Memory Screening** — Standardized cognitive testing and early detection using British psychiatric protocols
2. **Dementia Evaluation** — Comprehensive clinical diagnostic process to identify and subtype dementia
3. **Management of Challenging Behaviours** — Expert strategies for behavioural and psychological symptoms of dementia
4. **Treatment of Psychiatric Disorders in Seniors** — Specialised care for depression, anxiety, and late-life psychosis
5. **Cognitive Stimulation Therapy** — Evidence-based non-pharmacological treatment for mild to moderate dementia
6. **Family Education and Support** — In-depth guidance and emotional support for families and caregivers
7. **Geriatric Counselling** — Therapeutic counselling tailored to the emotional needs of aging adults
8. **Community Outreach** — Awareness workshops, screening camps, and stigma reduction programs
9. **Caregiver Training Programs** — Practical skills training for high-quality home-based care of seniors
10. **Senior Care Resource Hub** — Curated resources, tools, and referrals for comprehensive senior mental wellness
